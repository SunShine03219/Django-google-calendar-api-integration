Project Publishement

To find the right Partner who can help you to fullfill your project you need to publish a new project.

You have to big categories that you can consider as project type: IT & Services.

If it is a IT Project you the possiblities to precise which domains

  • Web Developpement
  • Digital Marketing
  • Infrastructures
  • Networking
  • Cloud Technologies
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Data Science
  • Software Engineering
  • DevOps
  • ERP Solutions

And for Service

  • General Administration
  • Finance
  • Insurrance
  • Legal Activity
  • Graphics and Design
  • Photography
  • Research
  • Coaching

Everyone in this plateform can see your project and has the possiblity to engage to the project

If you want to involove more partners to your project, you have the possiblity to duplicate your project.
